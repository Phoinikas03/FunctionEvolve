- Zero-order kinetics: dA_dt = c0 (constant rate, independent of concentration)
- First-order decay: dA_dt = c0 * A (radioactive decay, first-order reactions)
- Second-order kinetics: dA_dt = c0 * A**2 (bimolecular reaction of same species)
- nth-order kinetics: dA_dt = c0 * A**c1 (general power-law rate law)
- Reversible first-order: dA_dt = c0 * A + c1 (forward and reverse first-order reaction with equilibrium)
