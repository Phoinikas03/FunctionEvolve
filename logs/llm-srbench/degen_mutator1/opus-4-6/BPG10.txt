**Common formula structures in this domain**:
- Exponential (Malthusian) growth: dP_dt = c0 * P (unlimited growth, no density dependence)
- Logistic growth: dP_dt = c0 * P * (1 - c1 * P) (density-dependent growth with carrying capacity)
- Gompertz growth: dP_dt = c0 * P * log(c1 / P) (growth decelerating logarithmically near carrying capacity)
- Theta-logistic growth: dP_dt = c0 * P * (1 - (c1 * P)**c2) (generalized logistic with shape parameter)

- Allee effect (weak): dP_dt = c0 * P**c1 * (1 - c2 * P) (per-capita growth rate increases then decreases)
- Beverton-Holt continuous: dP_dt = c0 * P / (1 + c1 * P) (growth rate saturates at high density)
- Logistic with harvesting: dP_dt = c0 * P * (1 - c1 * P) - c2 * P (density-dependent growth minus removal)
- Seasonal logistic: dP_dt = c0 * P * (1 - c1 * P) * (1 + c2 * sin(c3 * t)) (logistic with periodic forcing)
- Time-varying carrying capacity: dP_dt = c0 * P * (1 - c1 * P * exp(c2 * t)) (carrying capacity changes over time)
- Richards growth: dP_dt = c0 * P * (1 - (c1 * P)**(c2)) * c3 (flexible sigmoidal growth model)
- Polynomial density dependence: dP_dt = c0 * P + c1 * P**2 + c2 * P**3 (general cubic density dependence)
