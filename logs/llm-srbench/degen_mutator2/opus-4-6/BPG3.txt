**Common formula structures in this domain**:
- Exponential (Malthusian) growth: dP_dt = c0 * P (density-independent unlimited growth)
- Logistic growth: dP_dt = c0 * P * (1 - c1 * P) (density-dependent growth with carrying capacity)
- Gompertz growth: dP_dt = c0 * P * log(c1 / P) (growth rate decreasing logarithmically with population)
- Theta-logistic growth: dP_dt = c0 * P * (1 - (c1 * P)**c2) (generalized logistic with shape parameter)

- Von Bertalanffy-type: dP_dt = c0 * P**c1 - c2 * P (anabolism-catabolism balance model)
- Richards growth: dP_dt = c0 * P * (1 - (c1 * P)**c2) * c3 (generalized asymmetric sigmoidal growth)
- Seasonally forced logistic: dP_dt = c0 * (1 + c1 * sin(c2 * t)) * P * (1 - c3 * P) (periodic environmental forcing)
- Exponential with time-dependent rate: dP_dt = c0 * exp(c1 * t) * P (time-varying growth rate)
- Power-law growth: dP_dt = c0 * P**c1 (fractal or surface-limited growth)
- Logistic with harvesting: dP_dt = c0 * P * (1 - c1 * P) - c2 * P (constant-effort harvesting)
- Beverton-Holt continuous: dP_dt = c0 * P / (1 + c1 * P) - c2 * P (contest competition model)
