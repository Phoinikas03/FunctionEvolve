**Common formula structures in this domain**:
- Zero-order kinetics: dA_dt = c0 (constant rate, independent of concentration)
- First-order kinetics: dA_dt = c0 * A (radioactive decay, unimolecular decomposition)

- n-th order kinetics: dA_dt = c0 * A**c1 (general power-law rate law)
- Mixed-order / Langmuir-Hinshelwood: dA_dt = c0 * A / (c1 + A) (surface catalysis, saturation kinetics)

- Pure concentration-dependent rate laws (no explicit t dependence) indicate autonomous kinetic systems governed by mass-action or empirical rate laws
